Key takeaways
FINDINGS
01
The Gap is Real
Indian developers earn ~87% less than global counterparts with identical skill sets. This is a structural, systemic gap — not explained by skill differences alone.
02
Language Matters
Go and Rust developers face the smallest pay gap vs global peers. JavaScript and Java — the most common — show the widest gaps.
03
Go Remote
Remote Indian devs earn 2.4× more than in-person counterparts. It's the single biggest salary lever available without leaving India.
04
Degrees Plateau
A Master's degree doesn't consistently outpay a Bachelor's in India. Skills and tech stack selection matter far more than educational credentials.
05
Gap Persists
Even senior Indian developers with 20+ years experience face a 79% pay gap vs global seniors. Experience alone cannot close the structural divide.
